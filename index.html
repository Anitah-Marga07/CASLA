Our Story

Casla Fashions started out in my mom's garage, where we'd repair and restore people's clothing. this went on for a while, then we started to make ankara pieces that became well loved and popular in our neighbourhood, so we decided to make it our primary service. Our garage became too small to accomodate all our projects, so we got a small business loan and rented out a space and bought some commercial/industrial machinery. We kept on growing into the brand we are today. I co-CEO with my mom and we look forward to the greatness our brand is going to.

Anitah Margaret- CEO

Cissy Innocent- Co-CEO
